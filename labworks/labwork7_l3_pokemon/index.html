• Bulbasaur

    Bulbasaur (フシギダネ Fushigidane) is a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It evolves into Ivysaur starting at level 16. It is one of the three Starter Pokémon that can be chosen in the Kanto region. Source
